# Avocado Toast

## Ingredients
- 3 slices sourdough bread
- 2 ripe avocado
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Red pepper flakes
- Salt, pepper

## Instructions
1. Toast bread until golden
2. Mash avocado with lemon juice
3. Spread on toast
4. Season with salt, pepper, pepper flakes

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Difficulty
Very Easy
