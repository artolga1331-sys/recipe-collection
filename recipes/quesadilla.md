# Quesadilla

## Ingredients
- 1 large tortillas
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- 1/2 cup cooked chicken
- 1/4 cup salsa
- Sour cream
- Oil

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in pan
2. Place tortilla in pan
3. Add cheese, chicken, salsa
4. Top with second tortilla
5. Cook until golden, flip
6. Cut into wedges

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Cook Time
5 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
