# Tomato Soup

## Ingredients
- 800g canned tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 500ml vegetable broth
- 100ml cream
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Basil
- Salt, pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic
2. Add tomatoes and broth
3. Simmer 20 minutes
4. Blend until smooth
5. Stir in cream
6. Garnish with basil

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
25 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
