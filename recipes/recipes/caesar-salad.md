# Caesar Salad with Chicken

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 romaine lettuce
- 50g parmesan cheese
- 100g croutons
- 3 tbsp Caesar dressing
- Salt, pepper, olive oil

## Dressing (optional homemade)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp mustard
- 100ml olive oil

## Instructions
1. Season chicken, grill until cooked
2. Slice chicken and let rest
3. Wash and chop romaine
4. Toss lettuce with dressing
5. Top with chicken, cheese, croutons
6. Serve immediately

## Prep Time
15 minutes

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
