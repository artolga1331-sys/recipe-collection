# Pasta Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g guanciale or pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
- Black pepper
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Boil pasta in salted water
2. Fry guanciale until crispy
3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl
4. Combine hot pasta with egg mixture
5. Add guanciale and pepper
6. Serve immediately

## Prep Time
15 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
