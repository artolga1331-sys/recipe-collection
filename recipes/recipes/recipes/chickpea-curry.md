# Chickpea Curry

## Ingredients
- 2 cans chickpeas
- 1 onion
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp ginger
- 400ml coconut milk
- 400g crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- Salt, oil

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger
2. Add curry powder and turmeric
3. Add tomatoes and coconut milk
4. Add chickpeas, simmer 15 min
5. Season with salt
6. Serve with rice

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
20 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
