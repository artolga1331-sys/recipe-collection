# Apple Pie

## Ingredients
- 6 apples
- 200g sugar
- 1 tbsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 egg
- 2 pie crusts

## Instructions
1. Peel and slice apples
2. Mix with sugar, cinnamon, flour
3. Fill bottom crust
4. Cover with top crust, cut vents
5. Brush with egg wash
6. Bake 45 min at 190°C

## Prep Time
20 minutes

## Cook Time
45 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
