# Smoothie Bowl

## Ingredients
- 2 frozen bananas
- 1 cup frozen berries
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup milk
- Toppings: granola, coconut, fresh fruit

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients until thick
2. Pour into bowl
3. Add toppings
4. Serve immediately

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
