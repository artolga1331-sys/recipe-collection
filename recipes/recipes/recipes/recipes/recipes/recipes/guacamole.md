# Guacamole

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1 lime
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 jalapeño
- Cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Mash avocados
2. Dice onion, tomato, jalapeño
3. Mix everything together
4. Add lime juice and salt
5. Garnish with cilantro

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
