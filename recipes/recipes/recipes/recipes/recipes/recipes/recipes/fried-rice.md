# Fried Rice

## Ingredients
- 3 cups cooked rice (day old)
- 2 eggs
- 100g peas and carrots
- 3 green onions
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 garlic cloves

## Instructions
1. Scramble eggs, set aside
2. Sauté garlic and vegetables
3. Add rice, break up clumps
4. Add soy sauce and sesame oil
5. Return eggs, mix well
6. Garnish with green onions

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
