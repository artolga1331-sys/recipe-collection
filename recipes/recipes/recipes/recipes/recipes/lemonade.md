# Homemade Lemonade

## Ingredients
- 6 lemons
- 150g sugar
- 1L water
- Ice cubes
- Mint leaves (optional)

## Instructions
1. Juice all lemons
2. Make simple syrup: boil sugar with 1 cup water
3. Cool the syrup
4. Mix lemon juice, syrup, and cold water
5. Serve over ice
6. Garnish with mint

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Very Easy
