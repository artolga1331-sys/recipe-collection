# Classic Hamburger

## Ingredients
- 500g ground beef (80/20)
- 4 burger buns
- 4 slices cheddar
- Lettuce, tomato, onion
- Pickles
- Ketchup, mustard

## Instructions
1. Form 4 patties, season with salt
2. Cook 3-4 min per side on high heat
3. Add cheese last minute
4. Toast buns
5. Assemble: sauce, lettuce, patty, tomato, onion, pickles
6. Serve warm

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
