# Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients
- 225g butter
- 200g brown sugar
- 100g white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 300g flour
- 5g baking soda
- 300g chocolate chips
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cream butter and sugars
2. Add eggs one at a time
3. Mix flour, salt, baking soda
4. Combine wet and dry
5. Fold in chocolate chips
6. Bake 12 min at 180°C

## Yield
24 cookies

## Difficulty
Easy
