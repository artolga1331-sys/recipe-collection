# Baked Potato

## Ingredients
- 4 russet potatoes
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Butter, sour cream, cheese, chives

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C
2. Wash and dry potatoes
3. Poke holes with fork
4. Rub with oil and salt
5. Bake 60 minutes
6. Cut open, add toppings

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Cook Time
60 minutes

## Difficulty
Very Easy
