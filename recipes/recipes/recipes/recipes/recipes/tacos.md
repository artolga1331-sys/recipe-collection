# Beef Tacos

## Ingredients
- 500g ground beef
- 8 taco shells
- 1 onion
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning
- Lettuce, tomato, cheese
- Sour cream, salsa

## Instructions
1. Brown beef with onion
2. Add taco seasoning and water
3. Simmer 5 minutes
4. Warm taco shells
5. Fill with beef, toppings

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
