# Falafel

## Ingredients
- 400g chickpeas (soaked, not canned)
- 1 onion
- 4 garlic cloves
- 1 cup fresh parsley
- 1 cup fresh cilantro
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Salt, pepper
- Oil for frying

## Instructions
1. Soak chickpeas overnight
2. Blend chickpeas, onion, garlic, herbs
3. Add spices and baking powder
4. Refrigerate 1 hour
5. Form into balls
6. Fry until golden brown

## Prep Time
20 minutes + soaking

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
