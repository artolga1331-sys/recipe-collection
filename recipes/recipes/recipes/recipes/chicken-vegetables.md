# Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

## Ingredients
- 4 chicken thighs
- 2 carrots
- 2 bell peppers
- 1 onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, rosemary

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C
2. Chop vegetables
3. Toss vegetables with oil and salt
4. Place chicken on top
5. Season with rosemary
6. Roast 35 minutes

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
35 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
