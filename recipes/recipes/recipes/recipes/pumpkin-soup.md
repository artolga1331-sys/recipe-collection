# Creamy Pumpkin Soup

## Ingredients
- 800g pumpkin
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 500ml vegetable broth
- 100ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, nutmeg

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic
2. Add pumpkin cubes, cook 5 min
3. Pour broth, simmer 20 min
4. Blend until smooth
5. Stir in coconut milk
6. Season with nutmeg

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
30 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
