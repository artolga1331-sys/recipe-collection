# Chicken Soup

## Ingredients
- 500g chicken thighs
- 2 carrots
- 2 celery stalks
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1.5L chicken broth
- 100g noodles
- Salt, pepper, thyme

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, carrots, celery
2. Add garlic and chicken
3. Pour broth and simmer 20 min
4. Remove chicken, shred, return
5. Add noodles, cook 8 min
6. Season with herbs

## Prep Time
20 minutes

## Cook Time
40 minutes

## Difficulty
Medium
