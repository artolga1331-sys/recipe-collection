# Rice and Beans

## Ingredients
- 1 cup rice
- 1 can black beans
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 cups broth
- Oil, salt

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic
2. Add rice, toast 1 minute
3. Add broth and cumin
4. Simmer 15 minutes
5. Add beans, heat through
6. Season with salt

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Cook Time
20 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
