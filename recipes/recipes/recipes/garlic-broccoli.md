# Garlic Broccoli

## Ingredients
- 500g broccoli
- 4 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt, pepper
- Red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions
1. Cut broccoli into florets
2. Heat oil in pan
3. Add broccoli, cook 5 min
4. Add garlic, cook 1 min
5. Add lemon juice and season
6. Sprinkle red pepper flakes

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Cook Time
8 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
