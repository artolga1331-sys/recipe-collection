# Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 200g flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 300ml milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter

## Instructions
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Whisk wet ingredients separately
3. Combine until just mixed
4. Cook on medium heat until bubbles form
5. Flip and cook until golden

## Yield
8 pancakes

## Difficulty
Easy
