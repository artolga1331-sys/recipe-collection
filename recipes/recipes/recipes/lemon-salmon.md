# Lemon Butter Salmon

## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 lemon
- Fresh parsley
- Salt, pepper

## Instructions
1. Pat salmon dry, season with salt and pepper
2. Heat butter in pan
3. Sear salmon skin-side down 4-5 min
4. Flip, add garlic and lemon juice
5. Cook 3-4 more minutes
6. Garnish with parsley

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Cook Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
