# Greek Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 1 green bell pepper
- 100g feta cheese
- 10 kalamata olives
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- Oregano

## Instructions
1. Chop vegetables into chunks
2. Add olives and feta
3. Drizzle with oil and vinegar
4. Sprinkle oregano
5. Toss gently

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
