# Scrambled Eggs

## Ingredients
- 5 eggs
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp milk
- Salt, pepper
- Chives (optional)

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, pepper
2. Melt butter on low heat
3. Pour eggs, stir constantly
4. Remove when still slightly wet
5. Garnish with chives

## Prep Time
2 minutes

## Cook Time
3 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
