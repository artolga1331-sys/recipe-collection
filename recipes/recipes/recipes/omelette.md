# Classic Omelette

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Optional: cheese, herbs, mushrooms

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs with salt
2. Heat butter in non-stick pan
3. Pour eggs, stir gently
4. Add fillings when edges set
5. Fold and slide onto plate

## Prep Time
5 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
