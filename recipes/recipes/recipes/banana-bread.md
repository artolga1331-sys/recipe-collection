# Banana Bread

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 100g butter
- 150g sugar
- 1 egg
- 200g flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 175°C
2. Mash bananas in a bowl
3. Mix melted butter and sugar
4. Add egg and mashed bananas
5. Mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt
6. Combine wet and dry ingredients
7. Bake 50-60 minutes

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
55 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy
